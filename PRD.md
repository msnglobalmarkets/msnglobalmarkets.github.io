# PRD: MSN Global Markets 2.0 (Futuristic Wealth-Tech)

## 1. Vision

To become the world's most transparent and efficient AI-managed investment platform, bridging the gap between quantitative institutional strategies and retail accessibility.

## 2. Core Enhancements (Q1-Q2 2026)

### A. Intelligent Lead Generation (Implemented)

- **AI Concierge**: Real-time business query generation via integrated chatbot.
- **Formspree Integration**: Automated lead routing to `msnglobalmarkets@gmail.com`.

### B. Futuristic Visuals

- **Animated Branding**: Transition to video-based logo dynamics (Deployed in Navbar).
- **Glassmorphic Dashboards**: UI/UX reflecting real-time data flow.

### D. Institutional Service Hub (Implemented)

- **Solutions Hub**: Comprehensive `/about` page detailing AI portfolio variety, balance methodology, and institutional consulting.
- **WhatsApp Direct**: Immediate client connectivity via WhatsApp integration on the contact desk.

### E. Advanced Portfolio Metrics (Implemented)

- **Multi-Month Audit**: Client dashboard now features stacked bar charts displaying profit-hisab across 12-month cycles grouped by broker.
- **Micro-Audit Sync**: Excel-to-JSON migration pipeline for seamless institutional data updates.

### F. Quality Assurance (Implemented)

- **Regression Pack**: End-to-end testing suite (Playwright) covering critical paths: Lead Gen, Portfolio Auth, and Mobile Responsiveness.

## 3. Futuristic Requirements (Roadmap)

### I. Real-Time Performance Dashboard (Phase 3)

- **Live MT5 Feed**: Integration with MetaTrader APIs to display live (delayed) trade signals on the `/performance` page.
- **Transparency Score**: A dynamic metric calculating the safety vs. return ratio of active bots.

### II. AI Strategy Sandbox

- **Backtest Simulator**: Allow users to "test" a strategy on historical data directly in the browser using a WASM-based engine.
- **Risk Profiler Tool**: Interactive quiz that recommends an AI Fund based on investor psychological profile.

### III. Client Portal (Private Area)

- **Strategy Toggle**: Allow clients to switch between "Aggressive" and "Conservative" modes on their own sub-accounts.
- **Weekly Insight Reports**: Automated AI-generated PDF summaries of global market shifts and their impact on the MSN portfolios.

## 4. Competitive Content Enrichment

- **Strategy Deep-Dives**: Detailed whitepapers for "Hedging & Arbitrage" and "Crypto High-Vol" strategies.
- **Glossary of Algo-Trading**: Educational section to rank for SEO on keywords like "Agentic AI Trading", "Dubai Fund Management", etc.

## 5. Technical Stack Upgrades

- **WASM for Client-Side Calculators**: High-speed financial forecasting tools.
- **Web3 Integration**: Wallet connect for crypto-based investment monitoring.
